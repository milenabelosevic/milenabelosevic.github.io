I am a linguist in the Faculty of Linguistics and Literary Studies at <a href="https://www.uni-bielefeld.de/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bielefeld University</a>.  

My research focuses on context-sensitive phenomena that rely on extralinguistic knowledge in discourse and interaction. I combine hermeneutic and computational approaches in research at the intersection of linguistics and the Digital Humanities.

Main research topics: 
  

-  discourse analysis, media linguistics 
-  conversational grounding in human-human and human-machine interaction
-  corpus linguistics, NLP, (digital) annotation  

See also [Research](Research) and [Publications](Publications).
